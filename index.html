After graduating from college, I chose to travel and expand my view of the world. I lived in New Zealand and Australia for 3 years and I believe this experience polished my communication skills and self-reliance. When I returned to Denver, I became involved in the Denver Art scene. I worked independently as a freelance Graphic Designer and helped run a small gallery, Forest Gallery, in Lo-Hi Denver at Forest Room 5.

I believe the evolution of design leads from construction to functionality to innovation. This idea directed my curiousity towards web development and transforming abstract ideas to tangible solutions.

I appreciate all forms of creativity and problem-solving and I understand the importance of long-lasting, mutually beneficial relationships both personally and professionally.
